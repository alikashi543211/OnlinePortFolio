Developed an HR Portal web app that streamlined HR processes and improved employee experience. Worked closely with a cross-functional team of designers, developers, and stakeholders to gather requirements, design, develop, test and deploy the web application. The HR Portal web app is a secure and user-friendly platform that enables HR personnel to manage employee data, benefits, time-off requests, and performance evaluations. It also provides employees with a self-service portal where they can access their HR records, update personal information, request time off, and view their performance evaluations.

Key Contributions:

Led the development of the HR Portal web app using technologies such as Ruby on Rails, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
Collaborated with HR department to gather requirements, design the user interface, and develop features that met their needs.
Implemented data security protocols to ensure the privacy and confidentiality of employee data.
Conducted user testing and implemented feedback to improve the user experience and functionality of the HR Portal web app.
Created technical documentation and user manuals to facilitate future development and maintenance of the HR Portal web app.
Provided technical support and troubleshooting to HR personnel and employees to ensure the smooth operation of the HR Portal web app.
